My name in Chinese is 全诗兰, which corresponds to Shilan Quan in English. I am a Ph.D. student in the School of Electronic Engineering at Xidian University, China. My research interests include brain-computer interfaces, multimodal fusion and neuroimaging analysis.

I am currently a postdoctoral researcher at Zhejiang University. My research interests focus on brain-inspired multimodal learning, particularly the alignment between brain signals and multimodal large models.

My Ph.D. thesis title is: "EEG-Informed Prompting for Brain-out-of-the-Loop Object Detection in Remote Sensing Images." Its Chinese title is: 基于脑先验信息引导的“脑不在环路”遥感图像目标检测.
